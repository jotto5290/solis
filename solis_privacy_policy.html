Solis (the “App”) is designed and built to be an offline-first, highly secure, and private mindfulness mood journal. This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled when you download, install, and use Solis.

We believe that your personal thoughts, reflections, and emotional tracking data are entirely yours. Therefore, Solis is architected with a strict privacy-first model: zero data collection, zero cloud synchronization, and 100% on-device storage.

1

Information Collection and Use

Solis does not collect, harvest, transmit, share, or store any of your personal information, journal entries, mood records, or usage telemetry on external servers.

  • Journal Entries & Mood Data: All text, feelings, reflection responses, dates, and streak statistics you input are saved exclusively inside a secure, sandboxed local database (Room SQLite) on your physical Android device. This data never leaves your device.
  • On-Device AI/Processing: Any intelligence, templates, or prompt-generation features operate entirely offline or locally using on-device resources. No search queries or journal text are uploaded to any external artificial intelligence APIs.
  • No Analytics or Tracking: Solis does not use third-party tracking software, advertising SDKs, analytics libraries, or telemetry frameworks. We do not track how many times you open the App or which features you use.
2

Device Permissions

To provide standard features, Solis may request access to certain system permissions. You have complete control over whether to grant these permissions, and you can revoke them in your device settings at any time.

  • Biometric Authentication (Optional): Used solely to secure your local journal entries with your device’s fingerprint or facial-recognition scanner. Your actual biometric data remains securely stored within your device’s Trusted Execution Environment (TEE) and is never accessed or seen by Solis.
  • Notifications (Optional): Used to send daily local reminders to reflect and journal. These reminders are generated locally by your device’s system scheduler and do not involve external servers.
3

Data Security and Encryption

Because all of your data is stored locally on your device, the security of your journal depends on the security of your physical phone.

  • If you enable the Security Lock feature in Solis, the App uses Android’s native cryptographic APIs and biometric frameworks to secure access to the interface.
  • To protect your data, we recommend keeping your device operating system updated, using a strong screen lock such as a PIN, password, or biometrics, and avoiding rooting your device.
4

Data Retention and Deletion

You have complete and absolute ownership of your data.

  • Self-Service Deletion: You can permanently delete all journal entries and application configuration files at any time through the “Delete All Data” option in the Solis settings menu.
  • Uninstallation: Uninstalling the App from your device automatically purges all stored journal databases, settings, and streak metrics. This process is irreversible, and the data cannot be recovered by us.
  • Data Export: Solis provides an offline backup and export utility that allows you to save your journal database file directly to your local file system or a physical backup drive.
5

Children’s Privacy

Because Solis does not collect, process, or transmit personal data from anyone, the App is designed to support children’s privacy requirements, including those under COPPA and GDPR. It is intended to be safe for use by individuals of all ages.

6

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. However, because our core architectural philosophy is based on complete offline privacy and zero data collection, future updates will never compromise your offline data sovereignty without your explicit opt-in. Any updates will be posted directly within this document.
